Kid A - Radiohead

Personal favourite radiohead album out of pure spite. I said I liked it back when I didn't understand it and now I do and I maintain that In Rainbows is objectively better but you know what Kid A is still my favourite. Ok Computer is also good and arguably better but Kid A is still the best.

Amnesiac is also something that I pretty objectively like more, now that Let Down isn't underrated anymore, Life in a Glasshouse is the new underrated song.
